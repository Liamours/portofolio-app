Show                                          | Not Show

WHAT THE BULLET FORMULA LOOKS LIKE

Pattern: [VERB] + [METHOD or TOOL] + [SCALE or CONTEXT] + [METRIC]

Weak:   Worked on a fatigue detection model for a company project.
Strong: Designed MultiscaleTCN from scratch on real mining fleet dashcam footage.
        Macro F1 0.78, 11% above the model already in production.

Weak:   Helped with bird sound classification research.
Strong: Fine-tuned BirdNET v2.4 on 12,511 recordings across 219 endemic and endangered
        species. Macro F1 improved 44.1% over baseline. Published at ICITACEE 2025,
        IEEE Xplore, as first author.

Weak:   Annotated data for a medical imaging project.
Strong: Annotated 600+ whole-body bone scan images under supervision of nuclear
        medicine physicians at Dr. Hasan Sadikin Hospital, Universitas Padjadjaran.
        Hotspot detection mAP@0.5 = 0.599. Patient-level AUC 0.956, surpassing
        EXINI and BONENAVI.

Weak:   Did systematic literature review.
Strong: Screened 212+ papers using Zotero and Parsifal, applying PRISMA-aligned
        quality assessment to produce a structured synthesis mapping trends by year.
